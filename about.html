OUR STORY

TehTea was born from a simple idea: to bring serenity amidst the hustle and bustle of daily activities through the perfect cup of tea. The website was originally created by a group of tea lovers who wanted to share their love of various types of tea. From soothing green tea to smooth and energizing matcha.

Over time, TehTea evolved into more than just a tea beverage catalog. The idea arose to offer products beyond tea, including coffee, milk, juice, and even packaged cold drinks. However, the name remained unchanged, as we would retain the branding we had when we first started the business.
